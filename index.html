Sourdough Bread

Tools and Ingredients:

  • Glass Container - 3/4 to 1 L (loose fitting lid)
  • Rubber Spatula
  • Kitchen Scale
  • Organic Unbleached Stone Rye Flour
  • Organic Unbleached All-Purpose Flour

Steps:

Creating the Monster

  1. Write down and record the weight of the jar (without lid if lid removable)
  2. Add 100g of rye flour
  3. Add 150g of lukewarm water (about 85 degrees F)
  4. Vigorously stir until everything is incorporated
  5. Cover with loose lid and label with date started

Feeding the Monster

Day 2 & 3
  1. Leave 70g of mature starter behind, toss the rest (this is why we took jar weight)
  2. Add 50g of Stone Ground Rye Flour, and 50g of the unbleached all-purpose flour
  3. Add 115g of lukewarm water (85 degrees F)
  4. STIR
Day 4 and 5 - Drop Water Content
  • 70g of mature starter
  • 50g of stone ground rye
  • 50g of unbleached all-purpose
  • 100g of lukewarm water at 80 degrees F
Day 6 - Drop Mature Content
  • 50g of mature starter
  • 50g of stone ground rye
  • 50g of unbleached all-purpose
  • 100g of lukewarm water at 80 degrees F
Day 7 - Drop Mature Content (Again)
  • 25g of mature starter
  • 50g of stone ground rye
  • 50g of unbleached all-purpose
  • 100g of lukewarm water at 80 degrees F
  • Repeat the process of day 7 feeding FOREVER
Notes
  • If you forget to feed for a day or two it will be ok
  • Feeding every 24 hours is perfectly acceptable, every 12 hours is better
